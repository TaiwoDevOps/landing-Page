About Us

Uvanest Company.

At Uvanest Capital Limited, we are an experienced team of professionals that combine a passion for Client Satisfaction and Industry research on the Fixed Income Markets. The company with an operational base in Lagos, the financial and business hub of Nigeria, was incorporated under the Company and Allied Matters Act 1990 in January 2016 and commenced operations in April 2016. Over the period, our trading activities and excellence has positioned us to maintain an array of clientele base which we serve using our service mantra ‘integrity through service’ as our watch word leading usa very vibrant trading relationships with key players in the Nigerian Financial Market space.

WHAT WE DO

We are a FIXED INCOME investment company and we support our clients in providing end-to-end: 

BONDS

Are Fixed income instruments issued by Debt Management Office (DMO)to raise Long Term
funds on behalf of the Nigerian Federal Government thus creating opportunities for individuals, institutions and other corporate concerns locally or internationally to enter into a risk free and fixed interest rate benefit investment
over a specified period.

TREASURY BILLS

Are Government guaranteed Short Termdebt instruments issued by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN). They are traded at both the primary and secondary market to
interest investors both locally and internationally. It is risk free and
duration of investment are stated upon issuance.
